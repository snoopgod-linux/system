Getting help with SnoopGod

If you need help, try Help from the menu, or the Help menu in most applications. Let us know about your SnoopGod experience at SnoopGod Community!

In addition to our extensive written help, the SnoopGod community in conjunction with the Ubuntu community, provides free technical support in person and over the Internet.

Learn more at SnoopGod Support.
